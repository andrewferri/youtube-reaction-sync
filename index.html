This web app allows you to easily sync a Youtube reaction with the original video. All you need to supply are the link to the Youtube video, and your own copy of the video.

FAQ

  1. Q. What happens with my copy of the video? Is it uploaded to some server?
    A. Your video is not uploaded anywhere. It is streamed directly from your computer to the web page in your browser. If you're familiar with JavaScript, feel free to take a look at the source and confirm. If you're not, grab a buddy who is and get them to have a look.
  2. Q. I'm not convinced, is there some other way to verify?
    A. There sure is! Go to the GitHub repository and download the source. Then run the index.html file locally or on your own web server.
